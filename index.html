System Overview

This system uses a Two-Tower Neural Network to recommend food products to institutional buyers (schools, kindergartens, hospitals) in the Russian B2B procurement market.

  • Buyer Tower: Encodes institutional features (type, region, procurement methods, budget)
  • Product Tower: Encodes product features (OKPD2 category, KTRU code, name, price)
  • Matching: Cosine similarity in 64-dimensional embedding space

  2. Browse Buyers to explore institutional profiles
